Learn More About Mental Health

Feeling stressed out, tired, or mentally exhausted? Are you curious as to what you're feeling is normal, or whether it's getting to be out of hand? Are you concerned about a friend's safety? Or just interested in learning more about mental health in general? Check out the resources below - you'll be glad you did.

Determine your Mental Health Risk Level

The PHQ-9 is the 9-item version of the Patient Health Questionnaire, and is well-validated and widely used by clinicians to measure a user's risk level for depression.

Reach Out to a Trained Peer Counselor

The RAP-Line (Reach A Peer Hotline) is a group of student counselors trained to help students struggling with mental health issues.
